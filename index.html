Idea

The linkage between the student and his teacher

A fully-fledged program accredited by the Ministry of Education will act as a conduit for communication between students and teachers, in addition to boasting numerous other features that will be expounded on in the features section. With sustained development and investment, these attributes hold the potential to catapult Egypt to the top echelon of global education rankings.

The complete control over regulating the educational process

With rigorous oversight through official regulations, the program could become a potent instrument for streamlining the educational process. This could be achieved through the dissemination of official news, monitoring the progression of education, and conducting direct surveys to elicit the perspectives of students and teachers.

Features

Direct communication between students and their instructors

Students shall be empowered to book one-on-one sessions with an instructor who specializes in the subject matter they wish to study, at a pre-determined time slot within the weekly school schedule. Subsequently, the school shall furnish a response to the student's request, either endorsing it or proposing alternative options to ensure their convenience and flexibility

The financial return to the state

The prospect of generating financial profits stands as a motivating force to undertake this venture. Ensuring its viability, it is imperative to have a version of the software for both Windows and macOS systems. And it will be accessible through the Apple Store and Google Play. This, in turn, will streamline the development of a premium version, complete with supplementary features, leading to a substantial boost in national revenue. Additionally, fee-based lectures will be offered on the platform. These, alongside other benefits, will greatly contribute to the nation's financial gain, resulting in higher salaries for educators.
