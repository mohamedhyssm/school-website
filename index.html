The Spread of Rumors and Fake News Problem

At the outset, I'd like one of the key features of this program to be the creation of a student community specifically by the Ministry of Education itself, which will disseminate official news on a regular basis, instead of relying on frequently manipulated social media. Each school could use its student community to spread important school news through an account managed by a school official.

A solution for the issue of private lessons

One way to tackle the problem of private lessons is by providing digital lectures. This would allow teachers to present the lectures online, and students would be able to access and watch them at a later time. It's imperative that this service is offered completely free of charge, as it's a fundamental aspect of a teacher's duties. We have a suitable proposal to compensate for these funds that would satisfy both parties; the state would receive financial returns, leading to an increase in teacher salaries, and on the other hand, it would benefit students. it's going to be unveiled in the coming lines. However, it's worth reiterating that the availability of free digital lectures should be the driving force behind phasing out private lessons.
